About Me

Aloha! I'm Lacey — a graphic designer currently based in O'ahu, Hawaii. I live with my girlfriend and our sweet pup, and I find joy in building meaningful design, growing community, and living intentionally.

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Web Design through the University of Florida, constantly expanding my skills in digital media, illustration, and frontend development. When I’m not designing, I’m running, cooking, creating art, or tending to my plants.
